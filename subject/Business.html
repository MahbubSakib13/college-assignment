Business studies

Studying business can provide you with a wide range of skills and knowledge that are essential for success in the world of commerce and entrepreneurship. Here are some of the key things you can learn when you study business:

  • Financial Statements:

    You'll learn how to create, analyze, and interpret financial statements, including the income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. These statements provide crucial insights into a company's financial health.

  • Entrepreneurship:

    You'll learn about starting and running businesses, including developing business plans, securing funding, and managing the various aspects of a startup.

  • Financial Analysis:

    You'll gain the ability to assess a company's performance by analyzing financial data. This includes calculating profitability ratios, liquidity ratios, and solvency ratios.

  • Budgeting and Forecasting:

    Accounting helps you prepare budgets, forecast future financial performance, and make informed decisions based on financial projections.

  • Economic Models:

    You'll learn to use economic models to simplify and analyze complex real-world situations. Models help in making predictions, understanding economic phenomena, and formulating policies.
